CYCLING


  • Cardiovascular Health:

    Boosts heart and lung function.

  • Muscle Strength:

    Strengthens lower body muscles.

  • Low Impact:

    Gentle on joints compared to running.

DANCING


  • Flexibility:

    Improves Range of Motion.

  • Cooordination:

    Enhances balance and spatial awareness.

  • Mood Booster:

    Releases endorphins, reducing stress.
